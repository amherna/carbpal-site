Restore a CarbPal Basic purchase

  1. Sign in to CarbPal with the account you use for the app.
  2. Make sure the device uses the same Apple Account that originally purchased CarbPal Basic.
  3. On the CarbPal subscription screen, tap Restore Purchases.
  4. If already inside the app, open Settings and tap Restore Purchases.

Restoring a purchase does not create a new charge.

Cancel CarbPal Basic

  1. Open the iPhone or iPad Settings app.
  2. Tap your name or Apple Account.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select CarbPal.
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription and follow Apple's instructions.

Apple manages subscription billing and cancellation. See Apple's subscription cancellation instructions for more help.

Delete a CarbPal account

Account deletion can be initiated inside CarbPal:

  1. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. If the subscription screen is shown, scroll to Account and tap Delete Account.
  3. If the main app is unlocked, open Settings, find Account, and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm permanent deletion.

Account deletion is permanent and removes the CarbPal account and local app data handled by the deletion process. Deleting an account does not cancel an Apple subscription. Cancel the subscription separately using the instructions above.

Sign-in and password help

  • Confirm the email address is entered correctly.
  • Check the inbox and spam folder for account-confirmation or password-reset messages.
  • Tap Forgot password? on the sign-in screen to request a reset link.
  • Contact support if the reset message does not arrive.
Safety statement CarbPal is a carb-counting and meal logging tool. CarbPal does not calculate, recommend, suggest, or prescribe insulin doses. Any insulin dose entered in the app is manually entered by the user for personal logging only. Always follow your diabetes care plan and consult your healthcare provider before making treatment decisions.

Medical or emergency questions

CarbPal support cannot provide medical advice or emergency assistance. Contact your healthcare provider for treatment questions. For an emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.
