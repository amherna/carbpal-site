Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CarbPal collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the CarbPal mobile app, website, subscription services, or contact support.

Information CarbPal may collect

  • Account information: email address, account identifier, and authentication information.
  • Profile settings: profile name or nickname, carb-ratio references, time-of-day labels, and app preferences.
  • Meal information: saved foods, serving descriptions, carb entries, carb totals, meal notes, favorites, and meal history.
  • Optional log information: an insulin amount manually entered by the user for personal recordkeeping.
  • Subscription information: product, entitlement, purchase, renewal, expiration, and restore status.
  • Support and usage information: messages sent to support and limited technical information needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the app.

How information is used

CarbPal uses information to:

  • Create and authenticate accounts.
  • Provide carb-counting, saved-meal, profile, history, and export features.
  • Verify CarbPal Basic subscription access.
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot app problems.
  • Protect the app, prevent misuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Storage and service providers

Meal logs, carb entries, saved foods, and profile settings may be stored locally on the user's device. CarbPal uses service providers only as needed to operate the app:

  • Supabase: account authentication and related backend or database services, where enabled.
  • RevenueCat: subscription entitlement and purchase-status management.
  • Apple App Store: payment processing, receipts, subscription billing, renewal, cancellation, and purchase restoration.

These providers process information under their own privacy terms. CarbPal does not receive your complete payment-card information from Apple.

Medical and health-related entries

CarbPal is a carb-counting and meal logging tool. It does not calculate, recommend, suggest, or prescribe insulin doses. Any insulin amount entered in the app is manually entered by the user for personal logging only.

Sharing and sale of personal data

CarbPal does not sell personal data. Information may be shared with the service providers listed above when needed to operate the app, comply with law, protect users, or complete a transaction requested by the user.

Data retention and deletion

Information is kept only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Users can initiate account deletion inside CarbPal from the Account section. Local meal and profile information can also be removed from the device through the app's deletion controls. Deleting a CarbPal account does not automatically cancel an Apple subscription.

Security

CarbPal uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards designed to protect information. No storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children and family use

CarbPal is intended for use by families, parents, caregivers, and teens with appropriate parent or caregiver involvement. A parent or legal guardian should manage an account used by a child under the age required for independent consent in their location.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as CarbPal changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when material updates are published.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to support@carbpal.app.
